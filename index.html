Video Player Demo Page

Have you ever wanted a single video tag for all your video needs? This web component is a custom video player that can handle YouTube, Vimeo, and self-hosted videos. It also has a custom aspect ratio feature. You can also add a poster image, if you want.

Video play events are normalized across all player types. No more figuring out how to listen for different play events in different players.

Keyboard accessbile.

                                <video-player
                                src="/cat-head-rub.mp4" <-- Can be a YouTube or Vimeo URL or a self-hosted video
                                allowfullscreen <-- Optional
                                aspect-ratio="9x16" <-- Default is 16x9
                                poster="/self-hosted-9x16.jpg" <-- Optional
                                playbutton <-- Optional

                                ></video-player>
